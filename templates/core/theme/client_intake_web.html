Additional Info

1. What are the organizational goals and objectives and how do you contribute?

2. If you have taken part in coaching in the past, what worked well for you and what did not work for you?

3. What do you hope to get out of this coaching engagement? i.e. what will be most different for you six months from now?

4. Where were you born and raised and with whom did you grow up, i.e. parents, brother, sister, etc.?

5. What schools did you attend (college and post-college)?

6. What is your current family situation? Are you single? Do you have a spouse or partner? Do you have children?

7. How would people have described your personality while you were growing up?

8. What were your best subjects in middle school and high school?

9. What extracurricular activities did you engage in (sports, clubs, jobs)?

10. In your career, describe one of the most difficult challenges you faced. Explain how you approached it and with whom you worked best and least well.

11. Looking back in your career and life, can you describe some successes or accomplishments that you feel the most proud of?

12. As you look ahead in your career, are there things you would especially like to avoid or are afraid of and why?

13. As you look ahead in your career, are there things you are especially looking forward to and why?

14. What are you really good at in your career? What do you have to contribute that is unique or what special knowledge do you have?

15. What is the compliment or acknowledgment that you hear most often about yourself?

16. How would you describe nagement style? What do you do best and what do you do least well?

17. What do you do when you’re really up against an obstacle or barrier?

18. When you are most “stuck,” what can I say that will return you to action?

19. How do you go about learning something new?

20. What approaches encourage you to motivate?

21. What approaches discourage or de-motivate you?
